PRODUCT

InventTech brings inventory, purchase orders, and approvals into one clean dashboard for textile teams.

InventTech is a web-based manufacturing and trading management system built to simplify daily operations for textile and garment businesses. Manage all your masters—parties/vendors, jobbers, materials, types, shades, firms, and locations—without messy spreadsheets.

Create and track purchase orders with approval status, maintain clean data across teams, and get operational visibility in one place—so decisions become faster, work stays organized, and follow-ups reduce.

Features

Built for real textile workflows—clean masters, tracked approvals, and clear visibility across purchase and inventory.

Centralized Masters

Maintain parties/vendors, jobbers, materials, types, shades, firms, and locations in one clean system.

PO Creation + Approval Status

Generate purchase orders and track approval status so everyone knows what’s pending and what’s confirmed.

Inventory Visibility

Track yarn/greige/trim/finished inventory with location clarity—know availability before you commit.

Fewer Errors, Cleaner Data

Reduce duplicates and mistakes by managing everything from structured masters instead of scattered sheets.

Faster Daily Operations

Quick search + consistent workflows help teams work faster with less manual follow-up.

One Dashboard, Better Control

Get operational visibility in one place—so owners and managers can review and act faster.
