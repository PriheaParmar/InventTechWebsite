PRODUCT

InventTech brings inventory, purchase orders, and approvals into one clean dashboard for textile teams.

Explore Features Request Walkthrough

InventTech is a web-based manufacturing and trading management system built to simplify daily operations for textile and garment businesses. Manage all your masters—parties/vendors, jobbers, materials, types, shades, firms, and locations—without messy spreadsheets.

Create and track purchase orders with approval status, maintain clean data across teams, and get operational visibility in one place—so decisions become faster, work stays organized, and follow-ups reduce.

Built for real textile workflows—clean masters, tracked approvals, and clear visibility across purchase and inventory.
